Hi, I am Tiffany Joy.

"Photography takes an instant out of time, altering life by holding it still" - Dorothea Lange

Most people just call me TJ. I am a professional photographer based in Christchurch. Did I mention that I also have a mild K-pop obsession?

I have always loved photography since I was a teenager. I grew up in a small town in the Philippines when my family moved to New Zealand in 2016. I gradually adjusted to the kiwi lifestyle and feel really privileged living in this country. Still, I remain a town girl at heart as well as my passion to take photos.

I am also a registered psychologist where I run a small counselling centre. If I not taking photos or helping people, you will see me in the playground with my daughter and partner. Yes, I am very family oriented. My family is my inspiration and they motivate me to become a better person.

I can describe my work as simple, natural and timeless. It is my aim to create images you will cherish for many years to come. I would be honoured to capture part of your family's story.
